How do neural populations collectively process information?


We are a theoretical neuroscience and machine learning lab broadly interested in how populations of biological or artificial neurons represent the environment, and how learning and adaptation shape these representations.

Our lab is opening in the Department of Neuroscience at Baylor College of Medicine in January 2025. Join our team!
